About The Band

Formed in 2023, The Tex-Hombres is a group of seasoned Austin, Texas musicians who’ve spent years backing major touring artists—and decided it was time to start something of their own. What began as a side project quickly became a shared passion: honoring the timeless sounds of country music through songs that still speak loud and clear.

This band is a tribute to country’s finest eras, with a setlist full of heart, twang, and toe-tappin’ country classics. Whether you’re a lifelong country fan or just out for a fun night, you’ll hear classics that bring people together—played with honesty, soul, and a love for the genre.

Expect a trip down memory lane with songs from legends like George Strait, Dwight Yoakam, Vince Gill, Merle Haggard, Joe Diffie, Alan Jackson, Toby Keith, Travis Tritt, Brooks & Dunn, Willie Nelson, and plenty more.

Meet the Band

  • Michael Matthews — Guitar & Vocals
  • Pablo Trujillo — Steel Guitar, Guitar & Vocals
  • Lucas Seiferman — Guitar & Vocals
  • Will Krause — Bass
  • Jimmy Milner — Drums

Whether it’s a packed dance floor, a relaxed night under the Texas sky, or your next corporate event, The Tex-Hombres are here to share the music that brought them together—and maybe bring you along for the ride.
